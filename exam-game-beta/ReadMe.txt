The Objective the game is to stand on the purple platform until the progresion bar is full. 
The player must also protect the zones the enemy from infecting all zones.
The player will have to hold off the enemies until they complete the task or the when time runs out.
Defeat waves of enemies by shooting the until all enemies are eliminated. 
If the enemy destroys all the zones it’s game over.

Controls:

Aim: mouse
Shoot Bullet: Left click

Movement: 

Forward: W
Left: A
Right: D
Back: S

---Menu Controls--

Restart: R
Exit Game : Esc

----Bugs---

Please take note of the prgresss bar.
The progress bar does not work properly sometimes.
It takes 10 seconds to complete the task.
